# Design System Notes

## Product Mood

SpotSignal should feel calm, trustworthy, and clear. Users may be worried when entering symptoms, so the interface should avoid alarm-heavy language, harsh colors, and emergency-style visuals.

## Risk Language

Use:

- Low signal
- Mild increase
- Cluster watch
- Human review recommended

Avoid:

- Safe
- Danger
- Outbreak detected
- You are infected
- This location caused illness

## UI Principles

- Keep explanations brief and plain.
- Show uncertainty clearly.
- Prefer aggregated zones over individual pins.
- Make privacy choice visible before submission.
- Use muted map colors, not siren-like heatmaps.
- Keep the personal result page reassuring and action-oriented.
